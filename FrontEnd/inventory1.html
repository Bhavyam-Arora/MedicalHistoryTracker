About Us

One of the unique features of this portal is its ability to maintain a student's medical history, providing a centralized record of their medical conditions, treatments, and medications. This can be especially useful for medical students who are required to undergo regular health checkups and vaccinations. In addition to medical history management, the portal also offers inventory management for healthcare facilities. This feature allows healthcare professionals to keep track of medical supplies and equipment, ensuring that they are always adequately stocked and readily available when needed.

Max Hospital is a world-renowned healthcare provider with a legacy of over three decades. With a network of over 14 hospitals in India and a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals, Max Hospital is dedicated to providing the best possible care to its patients. Max Hospital's partnership with BML Munjal University underscores its commitment to advancing medical education and research, and to empowering the next generation of healthcare professionals.
